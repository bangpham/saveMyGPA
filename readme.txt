*** READ ME ***
SAVE MY GPA! is a single person fighting game, inspires by Street Fighter. Player will
play as a student who fights Buzz in campus gain his GPA to a 3.0

There are 3 rounds of fight. Each round you earn 
1.0 of GPA which is reflected on your scoreboard.
First fighter to win 3 rounds, wins the game.

Attack with A
Defense with B
Jump with R
Cheat by holding L to disable Buzz's aggresiveness
Navigate with Left and Right
